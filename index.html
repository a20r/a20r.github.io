Replicant Profile

Alex Wallar is Co-Founder and CTO of The Routing Company — a spinout from MIT CSAIL that brings real-time algorithmic intelligence to public transit networks worldwide.

Before leaving his PhD to build TRC full-time in 2019, Alex spent years at MIT CSAIL's Distributed Robotics Lab under Daniela Rus (Director of MIT CSAIL), researching optimization algorithms for mobility-on-demand systems, autonomous fleet coordination, and multi-robot path planning.

TRC's technology replaces fixed-route buses with responsive, algorithm-driven networks — Uber-style pickups within the public transport system. Deployed across three continents. Backed by $5M seed funding.

Lemelson Presidential Fellow. MIT Diversity Fellow. Shell-MIT Energy Fellow. 10 research publications. 1,611 citations and counting.
